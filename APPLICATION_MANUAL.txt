ULTRA FITNESS - GYM MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
User Manual & Feature Guide
==============================================================================

Welcome to the Ultra Fitness Gym Management System! This application is designed
to help you efficiently manage your gym members, track memberships, and analyze
business performance.

==============================================================================
KEY FEATURES
==============================================================================

1. DASHBOARD OVERVIEW
   - Real-time statistics at the top of the screen.
   - View Total Customers, Active Plans, Expiring Soon (within 7 days), and
     Expired Plans instantly.

2. CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT
   - Add New Customer: Capture personal details, plan type, and validity.
   - Edit Customer: Update details for existing members.
   - Delete Customer: Remove members who have left the gym.
   - Profile Photos: Upload images or take live photos using your device's camera.

3. SMART SEARCH & FILTERS
   - Search Bar: Instantly find customers by Name, Email, or Phone Number.
   - Status Filters: View only Active, Expiring, or Expired customers.
   - Sorting: Sort the list by Name, Newest First, Oldest First, or Plan Validity.

4. ANALYTICS DASHBOARD (NEW!)
   - Click the "Analytics" button in the header to switch views.
   - Plan Popularity: Pie chart showing which membership plans are most chosen.
   - Age Demographics: Bar chart showing the age distribution of your members.
   - Business Growth: Line chart tracking new member sign-ups over the last 6 months.

5. AUTOMATED NOTIFICATIONS
   - In-App Alerts: Get visual confirmation for all actions (Adds, Edits, Deletes).
   - Expiry Warnings: The system automatically highlights expiring and expired plans.
   - Email Notifications: One-click "Notify All Expired" button to send personalized
     email reminders to all customers with expired plans (powered by EmailJS).

6. DARK/LIGHT MODE
   - Toggle between a sleek Dark Mode (default) and a bright Light Mode using the
     moon/sun icon in the header.

HOW TO USE
==============================================================================

--- ADDING A CUSTOMER ---
1. Click the "+ Add Customer" button in the top right.
2. Fill in the required details (Name, Age, Email, Phone).
3. Select a Plan Type (Monthly, Quarterly, etc.). The validity date will
   auto-calculate based on your selection.
4. (Optional) Add a profile photo:
   - Click "Take Photo" to use your webcam/camera.
   - Or click the camera icon to upload a file.
5. Click "Save Customer".

--- MANAGING PHOTOS ---
- To view a photo larger, click on the customer's profile picture in the list.
- In the view modal, you can choose to "Edit Photo" or "Delete Photo".

--- SENDING EXPIRY REMINDERS ---
1. If there are expired customers, a "Notify All Expired" button will appear
   next to the filters.
2. Click this button to send emails to all expired members.
- The application uses Chart.js for analytics and EmailJS for email services.
- Optimized for both Desktop and Mobile devices.
